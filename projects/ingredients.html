Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients, whisk in milk, egg, and butter. Cook on a hot griddle until golden.

Simple Tomato Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions

Cook pasta. Sauté garlic in oil, add tomatoes, simmer 10 minutes. Toss with pasta and basil.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt, pepper, lemon juice
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

Toast bread, mash avocado with seasoning, spread on toast. Top with red pepper flakes if desired.
